## üìä Hotel Performance & Revenue Analytics ‚Äì Multi-Property Analysis

This project focuses on the **end-to-end analysis of hotel performance data** for a **group of four hotels**, covering over **21,500+ records** across multiple dimensions 
such as date, property, market segment, and day type. The objective of this analysis is to evaluate **room nights, revenue performance, and pricing effectiveness** by 
comparing **actuals, budget, and prior-year benchmarks**.

The dataset includes key operational and financial metrics such as **Rooms Sold, Room Revenue, ADR, Occupancy Forecasts**, and **segment-level performance**, enabling a 
comprehensive understanding of both demand patterns and revenue trends across properties.
